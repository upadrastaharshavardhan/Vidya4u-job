Full Job Description
Purpose of the Role
______________
Responsible for overall HR Function covering Recruitment, Payroll processing,
Performance Management System, Training, Employee engagement, Administration and
Statutory compliances
Role Description
______________
  • Responsible for handling the complete recruitment life cycle for IT/Non IT positions
  • Develop action plans/recruiting strategies to identify qualified candidates through
various job portals and networking websites.
  • Maintain the hiring funnel and metrics like candidate’s conversion ratios, NPS etc
  • Regular Interaction with team and business unit heads to discuss requirements,
determine focus, sourcing strategies, interview evaluations, offers, hires, future
requirements and create a funnel of potential candidates proactively
  • Screen candidate on both soft and technical skills
  • Build strong foundation of culture, systems, and processes at scale
  • Ensure prompt resolution of employee grievances to maintain cordial managementemployee
relations and achieve dedication by employees
  • Negotiating and amicably settling disputes and maintaining discipline & harmonious
working environment across all employee levels

Requirement:
  • Post Graduate in HR
  • 2-5 years of sourcing experience in a recruitment agency or in a company.
  • Proficiency in various sourcing strategies including key-word searches and hiring
portals
  • Understanding of the recruiting funnel and monitoring of Talent acquisition
metrics
  • Exceptional communication skills
  • Proven track record of working in a fast-paced environment
  • Pro-active approach to launch new initiative and implement systems
  • Proficiency in MS office suit- word, power point, excel
  • Experience in working in a start-up is preferred
