About Company :
Haloni's success lies in its understanding of the harsh Indian climatic conditions and power quality and its unparalleled ability to design, develop and manufacture products accordingly. The company has a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Haridwar and has been accredited with ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015 and ISO 45001:2018 certifications. Haloni Technologies is one of the few companies in India, which has a fully accredited NABL self-certified laboratory and state-of-the-art R&D laboratory within the manufacturing plant that allows its dedicated R&D and design teams to push the envelope and develop cutting-edge products tailor-made for Indian consumers. It also has a pan-India service network for lighting to cater to the need of retail and institutional segments. Its wide distribution network comprising of over 800 dealers & distributors and 20 depots ensures that its products are made available across the length and breadth of a country.
